FAQ

What is Gloria?

Gloria is a communication platform that connects care staff with families, streamlining updates and ensuring transparency in long-term care.

Who is Gloria for?

Gloria is built for care facilities and the families of their residents, making communication easier and more transparent

How does the Gloria app work?

Gloria allows care staff to share updates and photos with families. It also offers secure messaging between staff and family members, as well as internal communication among staff. It keeps everyone connected, informed, and coordinated.
