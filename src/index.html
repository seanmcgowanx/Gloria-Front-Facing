FAQ

Who is Gloria for?

Gloria is for those who care about the well-being of loved ones in retirement or nursing homes, and for facilities aiming to enhance client engagement and transparency.

What does Gloria help with?

Gloria bridges communication between families and care facilities, building trust through transparency.

How does the Gloria app work?

Professional caregivers provide daily updates on your loved one's status, keeping you informed.

Is Gloria compliant with HIPAA?

Gloria is fully HIPAA compliant, prioritizing the protection of client privacy and data, just as we expect care for your loved ones.
